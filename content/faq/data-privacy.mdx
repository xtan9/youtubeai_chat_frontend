We process the YouTube URL you submit, the transcript we fetch or generate from it, and the resulting summary. Here's what happens to each:

- **The URL** is logged for debugging and abuse prevention. It's not shared.
- **The transcript** is held in memory long enough to generate the summary, then discarded. We don't archive transcripts.
- **The summary** is shown to you immediately. If you're signed in and saving to your library, we store the summary text against your account. If you're not signed in, we don't keep it after the response.

We do not train models on your inputs. The summary itself is generated by Claude (Anthropic) via our gateway, configured to not retain content.

For the full policy — including cookie use, analytics, and your access/deletion rights — see the [Privacy Policy](/privacy).
