Yes. From any summary view you can:

- **Copy** the full summary text to your clipboard with one click. The output is plain markdown, which pastes cleanly into Notion, Obsidian, Apple Notes, Google Docs, or any other note tool.
- **Re-open** a past summary. Signed-in users have a personal library of past summaries accessible from their account.
- **Share** a direct link to the summary view (the link includes the original video URL, so anyone with the link can re-render the summary).

PDF export and a markdown-file download are on the roadmap. For now, copy-to-clipboard handles most use cases — the markdown formatting is preserved, so headings and bullet lists land correctly in any markdown-aware editor.
