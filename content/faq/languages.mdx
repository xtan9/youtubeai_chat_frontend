The transcription stage works in 30+ languages — anything Whisper or YouTube captions support. That covers the major European, East Asian, South Asian, Arabic, and Slavic language families.

You can also choose a different output language for the summary itself. The picker in the summary view lets you pick from the supported languages, so a Japanese-language podcast can produce an English summary, or a Spanish keynote can produce a Mandarin summary.

Two practical caveats: very low-resource languages may have noticeably weaker transcription quality, and code-switching (rapid alternation between two languages mid-sentence) can confuse the auto-detection. If a result looks off, try selecting the source language explicitly.
