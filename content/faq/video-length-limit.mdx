There's no hard maximum. We've successfully summarized videos over 4 hours.

In practice, two things vary with length:

- **Time to first summary** — for a video with captions available, length barely matters; the response is usually under 30 seconds even for multi-hour content. For videos that need Whisper transcription, processing time scales with the audio length, so a 3-hour podcast may take a few minutes the first time.
- **Summary depth** — for very long videos, we structure the summary by topic rather than chronologically, so a 4-hour podcast and a 1-hour interview get the same kind of scannable outline rather than a flat narrative.

Very short videos (under ~2 minutes) are technically supported but often not worth summarizing — you can just watch them.
