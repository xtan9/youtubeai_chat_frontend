A few reasons a summary might fail:

- **The video is private.** Only public and unlisted videos are accessible to our service.
- **The video is age-restricted, region-locked, or removed.** YouTube blocks our service from reaching it the same way it would block any signed-out viewer.
- **The video is a YouTube Live stream still in progress.** Wait until the stream ends and the recording is published; then it works like any other video.
- **The video has no captions and the audio is too noisy or too musical for transcription.** Music videos, ambient/looping content, and very-low-quality recordings can produce empty or garbled transcripts.
- **You hit a temporary rate limit** (rare, but possible if you've been making many requests in a short period).

If the problem doesn't match any of these, it may be a transient issue on our side — wait a minute and try again. If it persists, email us at support@youtubeai.chat with the video URL.
