Yes. Every feature on YouTubeAI is free to use without a paid plan. You can summarize videos of any length, in any of the supported languages, as a guest or with a free account.

Signing in (with email/password or Google) is optional. It only adds personal touches — saving past summaries to your library, syncing language preference across devices — and it never unlocks paid-only behavior because there is no paid tier.

We may introduce optional paid features in the future (e.g. team workspaces, API access). The current free tool will remain free.
